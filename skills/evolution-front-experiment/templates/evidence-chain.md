## Notes

- This record follows the minimum required schema for the evidence-chain model.
- `reopen_trigger` records the condition identified before failure, escalation, or reopen.
- Probe entries should use the `probe_evidence` structure.
- If a reopen actually happens, the later reopen action should be recorded with the `reopen_event` structure.
- Freezing must follow the documented freeze rule rather than informal confidence alone.
